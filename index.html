A guide to solo travelling

First steps

Start by choosing a safe, accessible destination, booking reputable accommodation with 24-hour reception, and sharing your itinerary with someone back home. Pack light, stay aware of your surroundings, and utilize local transport or walking tours to explore safely.

Packing list

Essentials

  • Passport
  • Wallet
  • Phone and Charger
  • Emergency cash and/or card(s), kept separate from your wallet
  • Relevant documents, depending on the requirements of your destination (e.g. travel insurance, international drivers licence, vaccination certificates, visa paperwork)
  • Essential medications, labelled (remember to check for possible restrictions on bringing prescription medication into your destination country if needed)

Toiletries

  1. Shampoo
  2. Soap or body wash
  3. Toothbrush
  4. Deodorant
